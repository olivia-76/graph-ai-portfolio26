About Me

I am Olivia Keiser, a Professor in Epidemiology at the Institute of Global Health, University of Geneva. With over two decades of experience in clinical epidemiology and statistics, my work has focused on long-term antiretroviral therapy and HIV/TB dynamics in sub-Saharan Africa.

In this rapidly evolving digital landscape, I am currently expanding my research methodology to integrate Generative AI. My goal is to leverage these tools for task automation, responsible data analysis, and enhanced research communication.

Beyond the lab, I've spent years teaching "Evidence-Based Medicine" and tutoring "Problem-Based Learning"‚Äîexperiences that drive my interest in how AI can transform pedagogy and knowledge dissemination.
